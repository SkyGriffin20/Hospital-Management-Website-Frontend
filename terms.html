Med-Ease - Terms & Conditions

1. Patient Responsibilities:
- Patients are expected to provide accurate and complete medical history to ensure appropriate care.
- Adherence to prescribed treatment plans and medications is crucial for effective recovery.

2. Appointment Booking:
- Appointments can be scheduled through our official website, mobile app, or by contacting the hospital directly.
- Walk-in patients are welcome, but priority is given to those with pre-scheduled appointments.

3. Cancellation Policy:
- Patients are kindly requested to notify the hospital at least 24 hours in advance if they need to cancel or reschedule an appointment.
- Failure to cancel in a timely manner may result in a nominal cancellation fee.

4. Financial Obligations:
- Payment for consultations, treatments, and services is expected at the time of the visit.
- Med-Ease accepts major credit cards, insurance plans, and offers flexible payment options for uninsured patients.

5. Privacy and Confidentiality:
- Med-Ease is committed to protecting patient privacy and complies with all relevant healthcare privacy regulations. - Personal and medical information is handled with utmost confidentiality.


6. Code of Conduct:
- Patients and visitors are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and courteous manner towards staff, fellow patients, and other visitors.
- Any form of verbal or physical abuse will not be tolerated.

7. Security Measures:
- Med-Ease has implemented security measures to ensure the safety of patients, staff, and visitors.
- All individuals entering the premises are subject to security checks.

8. Feedback and Complaints:
- Patients are encouraged to provide feedback on their experiences through our official channels.
- Complaints will be thoroughly investigated, and the hospital is committed to addressing concerns promptly.

9. Medical Records:
- Patients may request copies of their medical records in accordance with applicable laws.
- Access to medical records is protected to maintain confidentiality.

10. Changes to Terms and Conditions:
- Med-Ease reserves the right to update these terms and conditions. Patients will be notified of any changes through our official communication channels.


By accessing Med-Ease's services, patients acknowledge and agree to abide by these terms and conditions. It is recommended to review them periodically for any updates.
